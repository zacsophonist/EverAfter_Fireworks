About Us

At EverAfter Fireworks, we believe your special moments deserve a magical finale. Our displays are designed to amplify the emotion of the moment, turning your already incredible day into one that is breathtaking and unforgettable.

Our teams of professional pyrotechnicians have created displays for everything from intimate countryside weddings to grand embassy galas. With decades of collective experience, we deliver stunning artistry and rock-solid reliability, ensuring your event is as safe and seamless as it is spectacular.

With teams based in Ottawa and the GTA, we proudly serve clients all across Southern Ontario and Québec. From Muskoka to Montreal, Maniwaki to Niagara, we’re here to light up your night with beauty, wonder, and just the right touch of magic — truly a storybook ending to your fairy tale night!
