About Ever After Fireworks

At Ever After Fireworks, we believe your special moments deserve a magical finale. Our displays are designed to amplify the emotion of the moment, turning your already incredible day into something truly breathtaking and unforgettable.

Our teams of professional pyrotechnicians have created shows for everything from intimate countryside weddings to embassy galas and live concerts. With decades of collective experience, we deliver stunning artistry and rock-solid reliability — so your event is as safe and seamless as it is spectacular.

With teams based in Ottawa and the GTA, we proudly serve clients across Southern Ontario and Quebec. From Muskoka to Montreal, Maniwaki to Niagara, we’re here to light your night with beauty, wonder, and just the right touch of magic — a storybook ending to your fairy tale night.

Wedding Fireworks

Seal your love story with a breathtaking finale! Our custom-designed fireworks displays bring to life your vision of a storybook wedding. From the first spark to the grand finale, your display is filled with magical moments that you and your guests will treasure forever.

Corporate Events
