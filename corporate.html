Corporate Event Fireworks

Elevate your corporate events, product launches, and company celebrations with a professional fireworks display that leaves a lasting impression. We incorporate your brand colors and identity into custom-designed shows that reinforce your company’s image for years to come. From the first burst of color to the dazzling finale, your display is tailored to reflect your brand’s unique story and captivate your audience.

Process

  • We can start planning anytime, even before you have a venue, giving you one less thing to worry about as your event approaches.
  • Reach out through this form to share your vision, and we'll start crafting the perfect show for you.
  • Once your venue is locked-in, we’ll secure all necessary permits with local authorities and our insurance provider.

Safety First

If your venue has any safety questions, please have them reach out to us. Our technicians are fully certified by Canada’s Explosives Regulatory Division, and we follow strict safety protocols to ensure a safe evening for everyone in attendance.

The Show Must Go On!

Rain or snow? No problem!
On rare occasions we might have to shift the launch time, or in very rare cases reschedule, if winds exceed 40 km/h.
